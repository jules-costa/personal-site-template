Stuffed Clams

  • Ingredients
  • Cooking Instructions

Description

This traditional Italian recipe will take your tastebuds on a trip and have you craving more!

Ingredients

  • Fresh, live clams
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Red bell peppers
  • Parsley

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Shuck clams, clean shells, and shells face up on baking tray
  3. Mix chopped peppers and breadcrumbs in a bowl
  4. Add clams, mix
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until golden
  6. Finish with chopped fresh parsley and serve immediately
